ArmA: Cold War Assault is a military simulator originally developed and released under the name Operation Flashpoint: Cold War Crisis by Bohemia Interactive Studios. The Operation Flashpoint name renamed with the original publisher, Codemasters, and Bohemia went on to develop another military simulation with the same engine - ArmA.
Due to a recent license expiry, Bohemia was allowed to rebrand Operation Flashpoint: Cold War Crisis to the ArmA name and brand, and this happened in the new 1.99 patch which was released to celebrate the game's 10th anniversary.
