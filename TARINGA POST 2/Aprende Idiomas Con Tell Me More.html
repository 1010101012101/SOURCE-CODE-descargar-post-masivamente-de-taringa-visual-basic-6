To learn Arabic, TELL ME MORE Arabic introduces the standard Arabic used in newspapers, television broadcasts, and between Arabic speakers of different nationalities. At the same time, an introduction to dialects such as Egyptian, Yemeni, Algerian, and Moroccan is also given.
