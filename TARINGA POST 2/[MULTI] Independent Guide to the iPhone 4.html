Independent Guide to the iPhone 4

Dennis Publishing (29 July 2010) | ISBN: 1907232311 | 260 pages | PDF | 84 MB
This comprehensive guide to the iPhone 4 - the world's most desirable mobile - is effectively the manual Apple forgot to supply with the handset. Covering all iPhone features and reviews for top applications, this is the only guide you'll need to make the most of your phone. If you've just bought an iPhone, this fully updated MagBook is your ideal introduction to the wonderful world of Apple's mobile technology. Split into two sections - a user guide and applications review - this 260-page reference not only reveals the easy way to use your iPhone's operating system, but provides details on how to optimise the iPhones great features, functions and applications. From mastering simple functions such as multi-media messaging, using iTunes, emailing and downloading and watching movies, to the more advanced such as video conferencing and synchronising your iPhone, this MagBook is your iPhone Bible. The second part of the MagBook contains reviews for over 200 of the best apps - remember, there's an app for almost everything. Whether you're looking to download games, receive news feeds, find out where you are using GPS or street maps, or chat with your friends online, this guide will help you locate and download the ideal applications for you.
