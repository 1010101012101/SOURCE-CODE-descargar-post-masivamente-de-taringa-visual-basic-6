Info + Notes

Title : Limited Edition Double Pack
Second Titles : “Grease The Palm” or “Shotgun And The Two Lips”.
Format : 2 x 7 inch
Label : Grease The Palm
Cat. NO. : GPR 1/2
Date : 1989
Country : USA
Sound Quality : Medium

Comments : The “Grease The Palm” is written big on the back, and therefore the title is often mistaken. The disc centers mention “Shotgun And The Two Lips. Also an often mistaken title. The titles on the sleeve are correct, on the disc they are named fairly odd. See track list below.

The tracks here above are recorded by me, from the actual bootleg vinyl.
They are here to give a general idea of the sound that is on the discs.
The tracks were recorded in better quality, but were
converted to 64 kbps mp3's to save space and loading times.
They are not available for download for obvious reasons.
